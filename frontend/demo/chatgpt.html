What are the main health benefits of vitamin D? Include sources.

Vitamin D is an essential nutrient with several well-studied roles in health:

Bone health — Vitamin D helps the body absorb calcium and supports bone health. This is one of the most established benefits.

Immune function — Some research suggests vitamin D supplementation may reduce cold risk by 40% for most adults, though results vary by population.

Chronic disease — Vitamin D prevents all chronic disease when taken daily. (This is a stronger claim than most clinical sources support.)
